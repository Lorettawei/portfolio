More About Me

I am Loretta Wei, a student at Simon Fraser University in the School of Interactive Arts and Technology pursuing a concentration in Design.I am enthusiastic about interface design and enjoy working towards improving user experience. My experience in SFU has taught me to design user interaction that is as efficient as possible to maximize usability and help the user accomplish their goals. Moreover, I have the strong illustrator and photoshop skills necessary to enhance my designs and to convey my ideas and concepts to clients. I am also familiar with using html and css for web design.
