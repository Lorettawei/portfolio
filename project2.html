Urban Space

- Research background of Vancouver Olympic village community

- Reuse a site of this area and redesign a building for residents living here.

- Decide to design a community centre here.

It is a project of group of 5. It spends our 2 months to start from research background of Vancouver and the Olympic Village neighborhood, go to site visit, make decision to design a community center, do design iteration, finish 3D modeling, and finish slides. My role is graphic design and space design, participate in designing slides and making 3D model.

Vancouver Research

At first, we did research the background of Vancouver including:

- North False creek

- Olympic village

- Granville Island.

Program

With the growing amount of young family in the Olympic village in the area, there is a need of a space where the adults and children can come play and learn.This area is too noisy and lack of culture and knowledge, so that it needs a quiet area where people from around the community can come with their families and children to converge. Our goal is to divide the noisy environment outside, create a sense of privacy, and have quiet spaces for reading and learning. There is an outdoor film venue which is a key place to draw people together to the site.

Design Iteration

We drew draft of the community center and made a roughly model of that.

We made decision of the final design and go deeply. We design the shape of the outside and inside of the building by their area function.

Final Model

Finally we created a 3-level community center including library, bookstore and study area. We considered our building as four parts, according to the study circle and circulation in the middle, the building was separated to two side. One is for quiet personal space, individuals are further away from the outdoor theatre. The other to allow fellow people to feel connected and provide a space to communicate with each other, while still having the opportunity to work on their own.
